WESLEY KIDS DAY OUT

At Wesley Kids Day Out, we're committed to providing quality, affordable day care for children and families in our community. This program was established over 40 years ago as a Christian outreach. We register children from ages six months through age 5.

Our instructors are people who love working with hildren, and who have the skills to help the children to adjust to socializing with other children without their parent.

Each day consists of various activities including: play for development of large motor skills, crafts that develop small motor abilities and creativity, free play, story time, songs, and grace before eating. Throughout the year you and your family may be invited to participate in church activities.
